Moana is about a story in the Polynesian island of Motunui and follows the story of a girl named Moana who is chosen by the ocean to bring back the heart of Te Fiti, the godess of nature and is accompanied with the once-mighty demigod Maui and they face many challenges such as Giant Coconut Crabs, a savage living cocolut tribe, and Te Kā a volcanic demon.
